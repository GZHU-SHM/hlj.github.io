I am **Linjie HUANG** from **Guangzhou University**. My research focuses on **surface damage detection, computer vision, and deep learning**.

My work is mainly related to intelligent inspection and vision-based understanding of civil infrastructure, with particular interest in image-based damage analysis and robust visual perception under complex field conditions.

# Research Interests

- Surface damage detection
- Computer vision
- Deep learning
- Vision-based structural inspection
- Image understanding for civil infrastructure
